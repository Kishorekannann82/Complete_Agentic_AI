Kishorelytics is a technology-focused YouTube channel founded by Kishore Kannan N, 
dedicated to simplifying complex concepts in Data Analytics, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Software Engineering. 
Through step-by-step tutorials, hands-on projects, career guidance, and real-world demonstrations, 
the channel helps students, freshers, and professionals build practical, industry-ready skills. 
Kishorelytics covers a wide range of technologies, including Python, SQL, Power BI, Machine Learning, 
AI Agents, RAG, LangChain, Azure, and other modern tools.
 By delivering easy-to-understand content in both Tamil and English, the channel aims to bridge the gap
between theory and real-world implementation,
empowering learners
 to confidently build projects, prepare for interviews, and advance their careers in technology.
